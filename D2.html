家醫登記

此功能可讓民眾登記家庭醫師。您可以在三天內隨意更換醫師，但之後只能每三個月更換一次醫師。

這項功能的好處包括：提供靈活的醫師選擇，讓您可以找到最適合自己的醫師；確保長期醫療服務的穩定性，避免頻繁更換醫師影響醫療效果；促進醫生與患者之間的持續關係，有助於提高診療質量。

警告：三天內可任意更換醫師，三天後只能每三個月更換一次。

Family Doctor Registration

This feature allows the public to register a family doctor.

The benefits of this feature include: providing flexible doctor choices to help you find the most suitable doctor for you; ensuring the stability of long-term medical services to avoid the impact of frequent doctor changes on medical effectiveness; promoting a continuous relationship between doctors and patients, which helps improve the quality of care.

Warning: You can change doctors freely within three days, but after three days, you can only change doctors once every three months.
