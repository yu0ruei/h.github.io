評價查詢

此功能可讓您查詢醫生的評價。查詢後，您將會看到醫生的簡介及其評分。此外，您還可以對醫生進行評分，幫助其他使用者做出明智的選擇。

使用者可以評分醫生，這不僅有助於提升醫生服務質量，還可以讓醫生了解患者的反饋，不斷改進服務。這種互動有助於建立一個更透明、信任的醫療環境。

Doctor Ratings

This feature allows you to check the ratings of doctors. After the search, you will see a doctor's profile and their ratings. Additionally, you can also rate the doctor, which helps other users make informed decisions.

Users can rate doctors, which not only helps improve the quality of medical services but also provides feedback to doctors, allowing them to continuously improve their services. This interaction contributes to building a more transparent and trustworthy medical environment.
