實體看診預約

此功能提供民眾預約實體看診的服務，讓您可以直接與醫師面對面交流，獲得更詳細的診療建議。

預約實體看診的優點包括：

  • 面對面與醫師交流，更好地理解病情。
  • 能即時進行必要的身體檢查和檢驗。
  • 有助於建立長期穩定的醫病關係。
  • 醫師可以根據您實際的健康狀況提供更精確的建議和治療方案。
  • 提升就醫的舒適度，減少對醫療的焦慮。

In-Person Appointment

This feature allows the public to schedule in-person appointments with doctors, enabling direct face-to-face communication and receiving more detailed medical advice.

The benefits of scheduling in-person appointments include:

  • Face-to-face interaction with the doctor for a better understanding of your condition.
  • Immediate physical examinations and tests as needed.
  • Helps establish a long-term stable doctor-patient relationship.
  • Doctors can provide more accurate advice and treatment plans based on your actual health status.
  • Enhances the comfort of medical visits and reduces anxiety about medical care.
